1 Level System

Single level system is ideal for beginners. Who dose not want to get into complexeties of typical accounting. Single level system enables you to maintain ledgers of all accounts, yo have the privilige of careting unlimited accountsfor un limited purpose and people.

You can also share your accounts with any one and maintain a proper dicipline in your financial matters. This system provide you all basic features except that, it is a single level system, where all accounts created are placed at single level. So all accounts created by you are placed at one place.

Best for simple business or home accounting, students, house wives, simple account maintenance, easy sharing of accounts with family, friends and or business partners. Besides, where you are comfortable using the single level system, you can easily switch to 2 level or 3 level system and vice versa, munshibaba.com provides you ease of switching from one system to another and that too very easily.

2 Level System

2 Level System facilitates users to create categories and create accounts in those corresponding categories. In single level system, users can only cerate accounts, in 2 level system a category is created and their account in any category is created. For example if main category is Bank, then in Bank users can create multiple accounts. In 2 level system accounts are categorised for more organised may of maintaining the accounts, This system is good for any one and is very easy to use. Besides it categories the major accounting heads. The system comes with already created major categories like Bank, Cash, Income, Expense. Users can edit, delete or creae more categories of their choice and creates unlimited number of accounts in there categories.

1 Level System

3 Level System is ultimate choice for those who want most organied manner of doing things. This system enables users to create a major category at the 1st level, then the head of account at the 2nd level and their particular account in that head at the 3rd level. For example if major category is Bank, then in bank there may be any particulaer Bank and in that particular bank user can create on or multiple accounts. Accounting was never so easy before munshibaba.com. Users always have the facility of switching between the system, like from 3 level system to 2 level system or 1 level. Besides, 3 level system organises your accounting in most simple way, there no other system can. The system comes with the most useful reports and easy sharing facility.
